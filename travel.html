Destinations

Maasai Mara National Reserve

Description: Maasai Mara is one of the most famous wildlife reserves in Kenya, known for its abundant wildlife and breathtaking landscapes.

Must-See: The Great Migration,The big five,hot air balloon safari.

Tips: Visit during the migration season for the best wildlife viewing experience.

Nairobi National Park

Description: Nairobi national park is the only park in the capital city it is most famous for its landscapes, scenery and the big five animals.

Must see: The Nairobi safari walk,animal orphanage,game drive,the ivory burning sight monument,bird watching.

Tips:opt for a vehicle with an open roof for the best unobstructed views.

Mount Kenya National Park

Description: Mount Kenya is the highest mountain in Kenya and offers stunning views and diverse wildlife.

Must-See: Summiting Point Lenana, exploring the montane forest.

Tips: Acclimatize properly before attempting to summit, hire a guide.
